#Results from step by step process and GTEx output 

**EFEMP1_Hernia_Ocular_GITHUB.xlsx** is the final excel output from the step by step guide.
- It contains three filtering criteria located at View > Custom views
- "Herniation_Criteria" are the EFEMP1 variants which have been associated with various herniaiton pathologies
- "Ocular_Criteria" are the EFEMP1 variants which have been associated with various ocular pathologies
- "All_Criteria" are all EFEMP1 variants which has been associated with any trait and inputted into the GWAS Catalog data base as of 20th july 2024

**GTEx_eQTL_Herniation.pdf** is the eQTL output from GTEx of all EFEMP1 variants associated with herniation pathologies 
**GTEx_eQTL_Ocular.pdf** is the eQTL output from GTEx of all EFEMP1 variants associated with ocular pathologies
